Eevolutions

Eevee (Japanese: イーブイ Eievui) is a Normal-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I.

It evolves into one of eight different Pokémon through various methods:

  • Vaporeon when exposed to a Water Stone.
  • Jolteon when exposed to a Thunder Stone.
  • Flareon when exposed to a Fire Stone.
  • Espeon when leveled up with high friendship during the day.
  • Umbreon when leveled up with high friendship during the night.
  • Leafeon when leveled up near a Moss Rock or when exposed to a Leaf Stone.
  • Glaceon when leveled up near an Ice Rock or when exposed to an Ice Stone.
  • Sylveon when leveled up while knowing a Fairy-type move and having high friendship.
